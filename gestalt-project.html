About the Project

The purpose of this assignment was to experiment with different aspects of typographic hierarchy, which include weight, linespacing, position, size and color, in order to effectively communicate the unique characteristics of a specific typeface. In order to successfully portray the typeface, we were required to do a significant amount of research about its history and its uses. Our goal was to clearly present our information and discover how typographical signals serve as visual cues for hierarchy and association.
